Nipsey Hussle (1985-2019)

Born: August 15, 1985 in Los Angeles, California

2005- Released his first mixtape "Slauson Boy Volume 1"

2006-Nipsey and his brother Samiel opened the Slauson Tees store in Crenshaw

2009-2014-He released numberous of mixtapes "The Marathon", "The Marathon Continues", and "Crenshaw" to name a few.

Fun fact: Nipsey made 1000 hard copies of two of his mixtape and sold them all for $100 each!

2016: Released "Slason Boy 2" his follow up mixtape to "Slauson Boy Volume 1"

2017-The grand opening for the original Marathon Store.

2018-First commercial album released "Victory Lap"

Children names: Emani, Kross, Kameron (Lil Wayne's son)

Longterm Girlfriend: Lauren London

Death: March 31, 2019 Nipsey Hussle was shot and killed outside a store that he owned called Marathon Clothing
